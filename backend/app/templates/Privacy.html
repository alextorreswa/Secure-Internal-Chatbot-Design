Privacy Notice

This Privacy Notice explains how Cascade Freight Systems protects employee data when using the Internal Compliance Chatbot.

Data Collection

Only essential login credentials and role information are collected to authenticate users and enforce role-based access.

Data Protection

Passwords are stored as secure hashes. All communications with the chatbot are encrypted and monitored for unauthorized access attempts.

Internal Use Only

Information accessed through the chatbot is confidential and intended solely for Cascade Freight Systems employees. Sharing outside the company is prohibited.

Employee Rights

Employees have the right to know how their data is used and to request clarification from the compliance team if needed.

Updates

This Privacy Notice may be updated periodically to reflect changes in internal policies or security practices.
